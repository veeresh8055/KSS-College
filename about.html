About Our College

The Kanakadas Shikshana Samiti is a pioneer Institution that bears the name a great saint and Universal Philosopher and renowned poet Kanakadasa. This is the brain child of our beloved President Dr. B. F. Dandin who was a popular professor in Statistics and Mathematics as well as a great educationist. He sacrificed his whole life for the betterment of this institution since its inception. For excellent educational service the Karnataka University, Dharawad has conferred on him the honorary “Doctorate” degree in the year 2011. His yeoman services brought him many laurels like Seva Ratna from HH Naganur Mutt, Belgavi. He is recently conferred with Karnataka Rajyostava Award by Government of Karnataka on 11.11.2020 at Bengaluru.

Our Legacy

The college started with the vision of imparting quality education to students from Gadag and surrounding districts. Over the decades, it has produced thousands of graduates who are serving in various sectors across the country.

With a strong emphasis on academics, cultural values, and social responsibility, KSS College continues to nurture young minds and help them achieve excellence.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a premier institution offering quality higher education, fostering innovation, and producing responsible citizens who contribute to nation-building.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that blends academic excellence with human values, promote research and development, and instill a sense of responsibility, discipline, and social commitment in students.
