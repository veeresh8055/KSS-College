Admissions

Join KSS College to experience quality education, a vibrant campus life, and career-building opportunities.

Admission Procedure

Admissions are based on merit following government norms. Students need to fill out an application form, attach necessary documents, and appear for counseling as per schedule.

  • Students seeking admission should apply for admission in the prescribed form available in the college office. They are advised to read carefully the prospectus before filling in the form.
  • Admission to any course is made for one academic year on the basis of merit and college admission norms. The admission is available to all on the first come first serve basis with the minimum eligibility as made required by the affiliated University.
  • Foreign students will be admitted to the college subject to regular studentâ€™s VISA and eligibility certificate from the Karnatak University. They should furnish passport number, clearance from the Police, documents related to their date of birth and permanent or local address.
  • Students coming from the Universities outside the Karnataka State, shall have to produce Migration Certificate at the time of admission.
  • All admissions made by the college are provisional and subject to confirmation by the Karnatak University. If a student after being provisionally admitted is found ineligible his/her admission will be cancelled.
  • Principal of the college reserves the right of admission whose decision will be the final.

NEP Admission Guidance

Under the New Education Policy (NEP), students have flexibility to choose multidisciplinary subjects with multiple exit options. Our admission counselors provide full guidance to help students select the right combination of courses based on career goals.

Eligibility Criteria

B.A., B.Com, B.Sc I & II Semester

  • The student must have passed the second year PUC examination of the Karnataka PU Education Board, Bangalore or any other examination recognized as its equivalent by the Karnatak University.

B.A., B.Com, B.Sc III & IV Semester

  • The student must have kept two terms at B.A./B.Com I/ II semester course of Karnatak University or must have passed an equivalent examination.

B.A., B.Com, B.Sc V & VI Semester

  • The student must have kept two terms at BA/B.Com III/ IV semester course of Karnatak University or must have passed the equivalent examination.

Eligibility Requirements

A student shall produce original/xerox copies of the statement of marks of lower examination issued by the PUE Board/Department of Vocational Education / Karnatak University.

Students are required to submit their Caste & income Certificate. Issued by Compentent authority. In addition to this five xerox copies of each certificate must also be submitted.
